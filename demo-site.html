What is the CUDIT?

The Cannabis Use Disorders Identification Test (CUDIT) is a 8-question screening tool developed by the World Health Organization to identify individuals with problematic cannabis use patterns.

  • Evidence-based assessment
  • Identifies use disorder risk levels
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Private and confidential
